First Meeting (And Ice Cream!)

On August 26th, 2014 the RIT Players will be holding their first meeting of the year. To celebrate this joyous event, there will be ice cream! Come old members and interested students all!

The entire Players executive board will be on hand to answer questions with Jerry Argetsinger, our faculty advisor; and Jim Simmonds, the director of Urinetown, the RIT Players main fall show.

Join the Facebook event.

Urinetown the Musical

The RIT Players are pleased to announce Urinetown: The Musical as our main production for Fall 2014. Urinetown is a satirical comedy musical about social irresponsibility, corporate overlords, and of course, bodily functions.

Auditions will be August 31, 2014 from 2PM to 7PM in the RIT Band Room. Check out the Facebook event for more details or to ask questions.
